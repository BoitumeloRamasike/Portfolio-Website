High Performance Computing - CSIR

July 2023 - December 2023 · 6 months

I participated in the CHPC Student Cluster Competition, where I worked with hardware from Dell to design and build a fully functional cluster. The experience allowed me to engage with South African universities, learn about high-performance computing, software, hardware, and Linux.

We focused on factors such as platform, target processing unit (CPU/GPU), memory, networking, storage, system dependencies, efficiency, and power consumption. My team and I progressed to the national round of the competition. This hands-on experience solidified my knowledge and skills in building supercomputers from scratch.
